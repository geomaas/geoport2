About Me

Though originally from New Jersey, I have called Charleston my home for the past 12 years and recently have made the plunge into the world of web development. It has been a pleasure to find a new passion, solving problems and creating new and exciting sites. It is something I truly enjoy. With newfound skills and old ones I plan on forging a new career and make something of myself in the burgeoning Charleston Tech Industry.

Interests

Photography

I have always enjoyed photography and videography, even as a little kid I always found my way to the family camera and that passion has followed me in to adulthood. I've been lucky enough to travel to some amazing places around the world and capture some great moments, but I feel lucky that I get to work on this craft in such a beautiful place as Charleston. I also work with several wedding photographers and working 10 plus hours on someones wedding day is worth to capture that one moment that these couples will remember for a lifetime.

Theater

I originally graduated from The College of Charleston with a degree in Theater, with a focus on scenic design. I loved how each show presented new and unique challenges to overcome, from the design to the rehearsals to the logistics of getting everything built. Theater is truly a collaborative art form and learning how to work with others is the only way of making it work. In my first couple years after college I designed 12 different shows and worked on nearly 50 others and worked several festivals as well. Theater is definitely a labor of love, but there is nothing quite like the rush of a live show and the satisfaction of pulling it off well.

Drone

Perhaps my favorite new hobby is taking my drone out. It's a wonderful way to see the world from a different perspective and it really has opened up new and interesting ways to take photos and videos. It's also just really fun to just zip it around and I am just constantly in awe of the technology behind it.

Travel

If I had the money I would travel all the time, but alas I do not. However, I have been lucky enough over the last several years to visit some pretty amazing places so I started making videos of my travels to better remember them. These videos are a fun way for me to relive these trips and they only get me pumped up for making more.
