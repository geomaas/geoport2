About Me

Though originally from New Jersey, I have called Charleston my home for the past 12 years and recently have made the plunge into the wonderful world of web development. It has been a pleasure to find a new passion, solving problems and creating new and exciting sites. It is something I truly enjoy. With my newfound skills and old ones I plan on forging a new career and make something of myself in the burgeoning Charleston Tech Industry.
